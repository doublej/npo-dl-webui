Single Episode

Download individual episodes by entering the NPO Start URL or episode ID.

1. Copy the URL from NPO Start
2. Paste it in the Episode tab
3. Click Download

Entire Show

Download complete series with all available episodes.

1. Enter the show's URL or ID
2. Select quality settings
3. Download all episodes at once

Season

Download specific seasons of your favorite shows.

1. Provide the show URL
2. Select desired season
3. Start downloading

Batch Download

Queue multiple downloads at once.

1. Add multiple URLs (one per line)
2. Configure download settings
3. Process all downloads sequentially

Getting Started

1

Configure Settings

Go to the Settings tab to set up your download preferences, including video quality, subtitles, and download location.

2

Find Content

Browse NPO Start and copy the URL of the content you want to download.

3

Start Downloading

Choose the appropriate tab, paste your URL, and click download. Monitor progress in the Downloads tab.

Tips

  • You can download multiple episodes simultaneously
  • Check the Downloads tab to monitor progress
  • Downloaded files are saved to your configured directory
  • Subtitles are automatically included when available
